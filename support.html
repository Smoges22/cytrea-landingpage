Cytrea support

How Can We Help?

Cytrea helps Adult Family Home providers and caregivers connect for hiring opportunities. Our support team can help with accounts, profiles, job posts, applications, messaging, payments, and data requests.

Last updated: May 2026

Contact Support

Email support@cytrea.com with the email address on your Cytrea account, a short description of the issue, and any relevant screenshots or details.

Expected response time: We generally respond within 1-2 business days. Some billing, privacy, or account verification requests may take longer depending on the information needed.

Help Topics

Account and Login Issues

Help with sign-in, account access, email changes, password issues, and account settings.

Caregiver Profile Setup

Help adding experience, availability, profile photos, credential images, and hiring-related information.

Provider Job Posting

Help creating, editing, or managing provider job posts for Adult Family Home caregiver openings.

Applications and Messaging

Help with application status, direct messages, notifications, and provider-caregiver communication.

Hire Offers, Payment, and Contact Unlock

Help with hire offers, paid features, contact unlocks, checkout, and Stripe-processed payments.

Privacy or Data Requests

Help requesting access, correction, deletion, or other privacy support related to Cytrea data.

Important Hiring Reminder

Cytrea provides tools to support hiring workflows, but users are responsible for verifying employment, credentials, licensing, references, hiring decisions, workplace requirements, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
